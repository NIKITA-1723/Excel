Project Workflow:
-----------------
1. Cleaned the dataset by removing duplicate records based on unique customer IDs.
2. Applied filters to explore and validate data efficiently.
3. Standardized data values using Find & Replace for improved consistency.
4. Created Age Brackets (Adolescent, Middle Age, and Old) using Excel formulas.
5. Built Pivot Tables to summarize and analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
6. Analyzed average income by gender and bike purchase status to identify purchasing trends.
7. Examined the relationship between commute distance and bike purchases using Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts.
8. Analyzed bike purchase patterns across different age groups.
9. Added interactive slicers for dynamic filtering by customer attributes.
10. Designed an interactive Bike Sales Dashboard to visualize key insights and support data-driven decision-making.
Key Insights:
-------------
1. Customers with higher incomes were more likely to purchase bikes.
2. Middle-aged customers represented a significant portion of bike buyers.
3. Commute distance showed a relationship with bike purchasing behavior.
4. Interactive filters enabled deeper analysis across customer segments.
